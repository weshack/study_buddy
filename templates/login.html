You never have to study alone again.

SciLi and Olin library are the goto places for Wesleyan students to study and plenty of students hope to find classmates studying the same material there. But sometimes the library gets loud or you don't find anyone to study with. Study Buddy helps you find a group of students that have the same goal as you: to collaborate and help each other study for exams and homework.
